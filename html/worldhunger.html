World Hunger and climate change

World hunger is not having enough food to feed the world. In this case people will have under 1800 cal per day depending on what food they have access to. Some of these foods would be healthy others may not based on the circumstances like money or environment. In today's age there is more than enough food to feed the World we live in but many people still go hungry. For a couple of years it was going down for the time being but, since then it has went up. A very important reason is climate change.

According to wfpusa.org climate change is the main reason world hunger is here. This could be the weather like storms taking down trees or making it hard to traverse the area like a flood.
