Impact of Climate Change on New York

Water Supply

New York City is known for its great water. Some call it the "Champagne" of drinking water in the State (source).

Climate change in the next few decades will challenge that. More rainfall will lead to higher levels of seawater in the systems the city draws from (source)

Sea-level Rise

One estimate for the property and damages cost of sea-level rise in the next 60 years is in the billions of dollars.

Storm surges will also become more prevalent putting people that live in the coast in danger (source).
